About Me

The Engineer Behind the Robots

I'm Karthika K, a Robotics Engineer and R&D specialist currently based in Kuwait, working at Meetech on cutting-edge social and service robotics. My work spans the full stack of robotics — from low-level sensor control and ROS-based navigation to high-level HRI systems and computer vision pipelines.

I specialise in humanoid robot development — including Pepper, NAO, Cruzr S1 (UBTech), and Alpha Mini — with deep expertise in secondary development, SDK customisation, and deploying robots in real client environments. Previously, I contributed to a 15% improvement in robotic assembly efficiency at HyperCube Motors, Virginia, USA.

Beyond engineering, I'm passionate about education — having trained 3000+ students and professionals across ROS, Arduino, and embedded robotics through 100+ workshops and structured curricula.
